* BULLSEYE COLA *
*****************
by Mercerenies

Thanks for playing! All controls are explained in-game, but here they are for convenience.

* WASD or arrow keys = Move
* SHIFT + WASD or arrow keys = Turn in place, without moving
* Mouse = Drag sodas to use or mix
* Backspace = Undo last action
* Escape = Restart room
* F1 = Replay room help text

CREDITS:

The game auto-saves in each room, so you can always resume your progress later. There's a total of 36 levels.
